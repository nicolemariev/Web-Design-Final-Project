About Our Firm

If you have been injured in an accident, we can help. Our law firm is dedicated to helping accident victims receive the maximum amount of compensation that they are legally entitled to receive. We handle all types of accidents. See our "Area of Practice" page to see the list of accidents we handle.

If you cannot come to our office, we will come to your home or hospital.

No Fee If No Recovery

We will accept your accident case on a contigency basis. This means we do not get paid unless we obtain a recovery for you. There is never a charge for an initial consultation or for phone calls or other contact with the attorney or staff.

Experience

We have been practicing law for over 25 years and have handled hundreds of personal injury cases. We give each case individual attention, and we keep you informed of the progress and status ofyour case on an ongoing basis.

Excellent Results

We have collected millions of dollars for accident victims. We have the expertise and resources to deal with large insurance companies and maximize recoveries for our clients.
