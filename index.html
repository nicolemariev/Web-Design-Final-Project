Welcome to the website for the personal injury law firm of Thomas J. Lavin

If you have been injured in an accident, we can help. Our law firm is dedicated to helping accident victims recover the maximum money that they are legally entitled to receive. You do not owe us any money unless your case is successful. We always provide a free initial consultation, and we do not charge for phone calls or other contact with the office staff. In the twenty-plus years we have been in practice, our firm has been successful in recovering millions of dollars for our clients. We have the experience and resources to deal with big insurance companies and maximize the recoveries for our clients.
